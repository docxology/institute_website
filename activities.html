Activities

Recurring public meetings, project sessions, learning groups, streams, and community events.

Activities are the Institute's live participation surface: learning groups, model streams, project meetings, public media, and recurring updates.

Open Participation

The PDF invites people to drop in to activities when it works for them. Activities are paired with public channels and project pages so participants can move between synchronous meetings and asynchronous contribution.

Learning Sessions

Recurring learning activities include textbook groups, RxInfer.jl sessions, Fundamentals of Active Inference, seasonal schools, and course-oriented work.

Media and Production

The public archive includes livestreams, podcasts, model streams, guest streams, symposium material, and audio-visual production work.

Project Sessions

Activities connect directly to Institute Projects and Ecosystem Projects, including project meetings, preparation workflows, measurement/reporting, and presentation opportunities.
