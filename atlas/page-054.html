Administrative
The Institute Administrative Unit performs various support tasks within The Active Inference Institute and the wider
 The Active Inference Ecosystem, such as project coordination, record keeping, graphic design, Grants, project facilitation,
preparation, Communications and compliance, and other activities.

Administrative activities contribute to the development of core infrastructure to provide such support and automate or
systematize and standardize tasks, and become the organizational umbrella for financial, human resources, Internship,
 Volunteer, security, community moderation and management, and related activities and organizational components. These
tasks are currently assumed by The Institute’s Officers, who will continue to provide oversight as the unit develops to include
more contributors.
