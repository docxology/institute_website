AI²C: Active Inference Alignment Coalition
A multi-institution initiative by Research Fellow @Mahault Albarracin to turn Active Inference into a platform for
AI alignment, governance, and embodied experimentation.

We are building:

• a research coalition

• a funding pipeline
• shared infrastructure for alignment work

Hosted by the Active Inference Institute.


Why this project exists
Alignment research today is fragmented.

It happens across isolated labs, disconnected disciplines, and without shared infrastructure or governance
models.

AI²C exists to fix this.

We are building a coalition where:

• alignment is studied as a multi-agent, organizational, and embodied problem

• governance is built into the system itself
• outputs are reusable, fundable, and collaborative


What makes this different
Most alignment work focuses on:

• single agents
• static benchmarks

We focus on:

Intrinsic motivation

• curiosity

• prosociality

Extrinsic motivation

• rulebooks
• norms

• safety constraints
