I am a Master’s student at the Institute of Neuroinformatics, a joint institute of ETH Zürich and the University of
Zürich. I completed my undergraduate studies in Cognitive Science at Aarhus University, where I began
developing Julia implementations of active inference models for cognitive modelling. I am interested in combining
computational modeling of human behavior with brain imaging to address questions about how psychedelics
modulate hierarchical inference, cognitive flexibility, and belief updating at the algorithmic level, and how the
ensuing computations are implemented in brain networks. Apart from that, I am an aviation enthusiast and enjoy
thinking about fundamental questions in physics and the philosophy of mind, as well as exploring the Swiss
mountains on foot or by bike.


Work in Progress
